# Delivery — Policies and Conditions

## Do you deliver to my address?

Yes, we deliver across all areas of Dubai, regardless of the district.  
We are located in Al Quoz and will gladly deliver your order to the address you provide.

If you have any questions about delivery, please contact us via WhatsApp: **{{support_whatsapp}}**

---

## When will my order be delivered?

We aim to deliver orders on the same day if they are placed in the first half of the day.

However, depending on the order volume and the time the order is placed, delivery may be scheduled for the following day.

Thank you for your understanding.

---

## What if I am not satisfied with the product quality?

If you are not satisfied with the quality of any product, please contact us by phone or WhatsApp: **{{support_whatsapp}}**

We will conduct an internal review. If the issue is confirmed, we will either issue a refund or replace the product.

---

## What happens if a product is unavailable?

If a product is unavailable, we may suggest a similar alternative.

All substitutions are always discussed and confirmed with you via phone or WhatsApp before being made.

---

## Replacement products or weighted items

When replacing products or when orders include weighted items, the final order total may differ slightly from the amount shown at checkout.

---

## What if I cannot receive the delivery?

If you are unable to receive the delivery personally, you can leave a note in the order comments asking the courier to leave the order at the door.

You can also cancel or reschedule the delivery by contacting us via WhatsApp: **{{support_whatsapp}}**

---

## How do you store products and prepare orders?

All products are stored according to proper temperature conditions and manufacturer recommendations.

We pay special attention to packaging quality and the freshness of every product.

---

## Why do product substitutions happen?

Our assortment is constantly updated and product availability may change quickly.

Even if an item was available when the order was placed, it may be sold out before the order is packed.

In such cases we suggest a similar product and always confirm the replacement with you.

---

## I received the wrong item or something is missing

If an error occurred, please contact us via WhatsApp or phone: **{{support_whatsapp}}**

We will investigate the issue and resolve it as quickly as possible.
