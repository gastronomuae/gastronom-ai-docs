# Payment Methods

We offer several convenient payment options for your order.

---

## Online Payment (Credit Card)

The easiest way to pay is directly on our website during checkout.

You can pay online using a **credit or debit card** when placing your order.

At the moment **Apple Pay is not available**.

---

## Cash on Delivery

You can request **cash payment when the order is delivered**.

For smoother delivery, we recommend preparing an amount close to the order total.

Typical UAE banknotes include:

- 10 AED
- 20 AED
- 50 AED
- 100 AED
- 500 AED
- 1000 AED

If possible, please prepare the correct amount.

---

## Card Payment on Delivery (POS Terminal)

In some cases we can send a **POS terminal with the courier** so you can pay by card upon delivery.

However, this option requires additional logistics and may not always be available. It can also **increase delivery time**.

In some situations we dispatch deliveries using external courier services to speed up delivery during busy periods. These couriers cannot accept POS terminal payments, so in those cases **card payment on delivery may not be possible**.

If you need this option, please confirm it with our team in advance.

---

## Payment Link

If needed, we can send a **secure payment link** for online payment.

This option is useful when:

- the customer is **not at home**
- there is an issue with **cash payment**
- a **POS terminal is not available**
- payment needs to be completed **after the order is placed**

Once the payment is completed, we will proceed with the delivery.

---

## Payment in Russian Rubles (Bank Transfer)

We also support **payment in Russian rubles via bank transfer**.

The process works as follows:

1. We calculate the order total in AED.
2. The amount is converted to RUB using the current exchange rate.
3. The customer transfers the RUB amount to our bank account.
4. The customer sends us the transfer receipt for confirmation.
5. After confirmation, we proceed with delivery.

## Refunds

If a refund is required, we process it using the same payment method whenever possible.

For **online payments or payment links**, refunds are processed through our payment provider.

Refunds may take **approximately 3–7 business days** to appear on the customer's bank card depending on the issuing bank.

Refunds may be issued:

- in full (for cancelled orders)
- partially (for missing or replaced items)

If you have any questions regarding refunds, please contact us via WhatsApp: **{{support_whatsapp}}**

# Internal AI Instructions

When customers ask about payment options, list the available methods clearly:

- Online credit card payment on the website
- Cash on delivery
- Card payment on delivery (POS terminal, limited availability)
- Payment link
- RUB bank transfer

If a customer prefers **RUB payment**, provide the bank details and explain that the amount will be converted using the current exchange rate.

If a customer asks about refunds after online payment, explain that refunds are processed through the payment provider and typically take **3–7 business days**.
