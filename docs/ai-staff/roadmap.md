
# AI Staff – Capability Roadmap

This document describes the planned evolution of the AI Staff system.

AI Staff will gradually evolve from a communication assistant into a broader digital operations agent while maintaining strict governance and human supervision.

---

# Phase 1 – Communication Assistant (Months 1–2)

Primary focus: customer communication automation.

Capabilities:

- Email classification and routing
- WhatsApp message classification
- Instagram DM capture and classification
- Automated replies using templates
- Telegram escalation for complex cases
- Centralized conversation logging in Airtable

Goal:

Create a reliable AI-supported customer communication layer across all channels.

---

# Phase 2 – Stock Awareness (Months 3–4)

Primary focus: operational awareness of product availability.

Capabilities:

- Shopify API integration
- Real‑time stock availability checks
- Detection of website vs warehouse stock mismatches
- Automated alerts when products become unavailable
- Stock discrepancy notifications

Goal:

Allow the AI system to answer customer questions about stock availability and detect operational issues early.

---

# Phase 3 – Supply Chain Assistant (Months 4–6)

Primary focus: supplier communication and supply chain awareness.

Capabilities:

- Supplier inquiry automation
- Tracking supplier responses
- Identifying delayed shipments
- Escalating supplier issues to Telegram
- Supplier catalog intake monitoring

Goal:

Provide early warning signals when supply chain issues occur.

---

# Phase 4 – Monitoring Agent (Months 6–9)

Primary focus: digital infrastructure monitoring.

Capabilities:

- Website product page monitoring
- Detection of broken pages
- Detection of missing product images
- Catalog inconsistency detection
- Weekly operational health reports

Goal:

Ensure the digital storefront remains operational and consistent.

---

# Phase 5 – Content & Growth Assistant (Months 9–12)

Primary focus: supporting digital growth and catalog expansion.

Capabilities:

- Drafting new product pages
- SEO suggestion automation
- Catalog consistency analysis
- Product description generation assistance
- Growth opportunity identification

Goal:

Support content expansion and growth initiatives while maintaining brand and operational consistency.

---

# Long‑Term Vision

AI Staff evolves into a **24/7 semi‑autonomous operations assistant** capable of:

- Managing customer communication
- Monitoring operational health
- Supporting supply chain awareness
- Assisting digital growth initiatives

All actions remain governed by **human oversight and defined escalation rules**.
